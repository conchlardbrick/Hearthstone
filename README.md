## What Is Hearthstone?

Hearthstone is a free-to-play digital collectible card game developed by Blizzard Entertainment. Inspired by the characters, spells, and lore of the Warcraft universe, the game pits players against one another in strategic one-on-one battles.

Each player uses a customized deck of cards representing:

* Minions
* Spells
* Weapons
* Special abilities

The objective is simple:

Reduce your opponent's Hero health from 30 to zero before they do the same to you.

Although the rules are straightforward, mastering Hearthstone requires strategic thinking, resource management, and adaptability.

## How Hearthstone Works

At its core, Hearthstone is a turn-based card game.

Players alternate turns while using mana crystals to play cards from their hand.

The typical flow of a match includes:

1. Drawing cards.
2. Spending mana.
3. Summoning minions.
4. Casting spells.
5. Attacking opponents.
6. Planning future turns.

Success depends on efficient decision-making and anticipating your opponent's strategy.
